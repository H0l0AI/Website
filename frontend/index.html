Making Life Better for our Four-Legged Friends

According to the ASPCA, around 3.1 million dogs enter animal shelters each year in the U.S. alone. Of these, approximately 390,000 are euthanized. Add this to the estimated total of 200 million strays currently in existence around the globe, and it quickly becomes apparent that these animals are in need of our help.

At Akita Inu, we’re committed to playing our part. We pledge to make direct donations to shelters, organize community fundraising initiatives, sell exclusive NFTs, and do a whole lot more besides to make life better for our four-legged friends, wherever they may be in the world.
